D E S I G N S

I started designing my own shirts in 2015 at the Spudnik studio

I design the images using Photoshop, then bring the files down to the studio where I can turn the designs into screens. I bring the screens back home and use them to hand print onto cotton t-shirts in my kitchen. I've also done sweatshirts, denim jackets, and pillow cases. I'm colorblind, so go easy on me.

I'm always up to something.
